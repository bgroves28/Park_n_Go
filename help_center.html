FAQs

Can I cancel my parking space?

Yes. You must cancel 2 hours prior to the event in order to get a full refund. You will receive your refund in 2-3 business days.

How do I find where I parked?

After you reserve a parking space, an email with parking directions and other information will be sent to you. You can also use the parking address link (found in “Your Parking History”) to input into Google or Apple Maps.

Do I have a designated spot in the lot?

No. You reserve a spot within the lot, but do not have a designated parking space.

How do I get into the lot?

Show your parking receipt to the lot attendant when you arrive.
